Recipies

Espresso (Italy)

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce finely ground coffee beans
  • 1.5 ounces of water

How to make

  1. Preheat your espresso machine.
  2. Add finely ground coffee to the portafilter.
  3. Tamp the coffee to level it.
  4. Brew with 1.5 ounces of water for about 25-30 seconds.
  5. Serve immediately.

Cappuccino (Italy)

Ingredients

  • 1 shot (1 ounce) of espresso
  • 6 ounces of milk

How to make

  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso.
  2. Steam 6 ounces of milk until it doubles in volume.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam.
  4. Top with a dollop of foamed milk.
  5. Optionally, sprinkle with cocoa powder.

Americano (USA)

Ingredients

  • 1 shot (1 ounce) of espresso
  • 6 ounces of hot water

How to make

  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso.
  2. Add 6 ounces of hot water to the espresso.
  3. Stir gently and serve.

Latte (Italy)

Ingredients

  • 1 shot (1 ounce) of espresso
  • 8 ounces of milk

How to make

  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso.
  2. Steam 8 ounces of milk until it's hot and frothy.
  3. Pour the steamed milk over the espresso.
  4. Serve with a thin layer of foam on top.
